Assessment privacy notice

Version 2026-07-09

Who is responsible for your data?

Advancy, 42 rue Washington, 75008 Paris, France, is the data controller for this internal training assessment. Questions or rights requests should be directed through Advancy's established internal privacy channels.

Why is the data used?

The data is used to administer professional training, record completion and results, support responsible use of AI, and improve training materials. Processing takes place within the applicable employment and data-protection legal framework. Advancy's DPO and HR owner must validate the final legal basis and assessment use before launch. Results are not used for automated employment decisions.

What is collected?

  • Identity and work email supplied through protected registration or by the authorized cohort administrator.
  • Selected Normal or Advanced mode, answer choices, server-calculated Charter/module and overall results, attempt number, and submission times.
  • Privacy-notice version acknowledged for the attempt.
  • Optional training ratings and comments, if you choose to provide them.

Your invitation or protected registration credential is captured from the link and immediately removed from the browser address. It is held only in session storage for the current browser tab. A random registration transaction identifier is stored beside the registration credential so that a reload or interrupted request reuses the same registration; both are removed after exchange for a participant invitation or when an invalid access link is opened. Answer progress, a pseudonymous session identifier, and a duplicate-submission identifier may be retained locally for up to seven days so that an interrupted attempt can be resumed. To retry an interrupted save safely, the same browser-tab session may temporarily retain the exact pending submission, including answers and any optional feedback; it is removed after confirmed recording, recovery, or restart. Identity and optional feedback are not placed in seven-day local progress storage.

The assessment application does not store your IP address, browser user-agent, full page URL, correct-answer key, or uploaded files in its results database. Do not enter client, transaction, personal, confidential, or market-sensitive information in optional comments.

Who can access it?

Access is limited to authorized Advancy training and IT administrators who need it for the purposes above. GitHub provides the static website and Cloudflare provides the secured API and database infrastructure. Service providers may retain their own limited security and operational logs under their applicable terms.

Where provider operations involve a country outside the European Economic Area, Advancy applies the safeguards described in its general privacy policy. Before participant data is imported, the production results database must be created and verified with Cloudflare's European Union jurisdiction restriction.

How long is it kept?

Participant and assessment data is scheduled for deletion no later than 365 days after the authorized cohort closes. The cohort may use a shorter DPO/HR-approved period. A longer period requires a separately approved purpose and privacy-notice version; the current system will reject it. Deleted data may remain in protected point-in-time recovery data for Cloudflare's applicable recovery window before expiring.

Your choices and rights

The identity and assessment answers are required to record completion. Training feedback is optional. Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or object to processing through Advancy's established internal privacy channels. You may also contact the competent supervisory authority.

For general information, see the Advancy privacy policy.
